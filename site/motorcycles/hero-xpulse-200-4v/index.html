New Owner Tips

Quick tips for new Xpulse 200 4V owners.

⚙

First Service

Schedule your first service at Every 3,000 km. Do not skip it — the first service breaks in the engine properly.

Engine Oil Guide →
💡

Break-In Period

Keep RPMs under 4000 for the first 500 km. Vary your speed, do not cruise at constant RPM, and avoid hard acceleration. This sets up your engine for years of reliable service.

🔧

Basic Maintenance

Check tyre pressure weekly. A properly inflated tyre improves mileage, handling, and safety. Keep the chain clean and lubed every 500 km.

Tyre Pressure Guide →
💳

Insurance

Get comprehensive insurance for at least the first year. Third-party only covers damage to others, not your bike. Add zero-depreciation if available.

Buying Tips

🚀 Buy First

These accessories should be your top priority:

  • ✓ Helmet (adventure/dual-sport)
  • ✓ Crash Guard
  • ✓ Tyre Inflator

✗ Avoid

Don't waste money on these:

  • ✗ Street tyres for serious off-road use
  • ✗ Cheap crash bars (they bend, not protect)
  • ✗ Universal-fit windscreens (poor fitment)

💰 Money-Saving Tips

  • ✓ Buy during festive season sales (Diwali, Republic Day) for 15-30% discounts
  • ✓ Start with essentials, add accessories as you identify actual needs
  • ✓ Check Amazon, Flipkart, and local dealers — prices vary significantly

⚠ Common Mistakes

  • ✗ Using road tyres for off-road — they cannot handle trails
  • ✗ Skipping crash protection — adventure bikes fall, it is part of the sport
  • ✗ Overloading for tours — weight affects handling significantly
  • ✗ Not reading the owner's manual for service intervals
📦 OEM vs Aftermarket

OEM accessories fit perfectly but cost more. For the Xpulse 200 4V, aftermarket offers good value for non-critical items like phone mounts and bike covers. For crash protection, go OEM or from a trusted brand.
