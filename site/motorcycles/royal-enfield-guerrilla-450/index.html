Frequently Asked Questions

The Royal Enfield Guerrilla 450 is priced at 2,49,000 (ex-showroom). On-road prices may vary depending on your city and variant.

The Royal Enfield Guerrilla 450 offers a mileage of approximately 28 kmpl.

The Guerrilla 450 is primarily designed for city commuting and short weekend rides. However, with proper accessories like a comfortable seat and windscreen, it can handle moderate long-distance touring.
