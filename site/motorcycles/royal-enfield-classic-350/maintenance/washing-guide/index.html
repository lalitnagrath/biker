How to Wash Your Motorcycle

Regular washing keeps your motorcycle looking great and helps prevent rust and corrosion.

What You Need

  • Motorcycle shampoo
  • Microfiber cloths
  • Soft sponge
  • Water hose

Steps

  1. Cool the engine
  2. Rinse from top to bottom
  3. Apply shampoo with sponge
  4. Clean chain separately
  5. Rinse thoroughly
  6. Dry with microfiber cloth
  7. Apply wax or polish

Tips

  • Never wash in direct sunlight
  • Use motorcycle-specific cleaners
  • Don't spray directly on electricals
