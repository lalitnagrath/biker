Chain Maintenance

Regular chain maintenance is essential for your motorcycle's performance and safety. A well-maintained chain ensures smooth power delivery and extends the life of your sprockets.

How Often to Clean

  • City riding: Every 500 km
  • Highway touring: Every 300 km
  • Dusty conditions: Every 200 km

Steps

  1. Put bike on center stand
  2. Apply chain cleaner
  3. Scrub gently with brush
  4. Dry thoroughly
  5. Apply chain lubricant
  6. Check tension

Recommended Products

  • Chain Cleaner: Motul Chain Clean
  • Chain Lube: Bajaj Tempo Chain Lube
  • Brush: Soft bristle brush
