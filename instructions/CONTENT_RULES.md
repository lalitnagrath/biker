# Content Rules

Every page must answer the user's question first.

Use this hierarchy:

Question

↓

Answer

↓

Explanation

↓

Recommendations

↓

Products

Products should never appear before the content.

---

## Motorcycle Pages

Generate:

- Overview
- Specifications
- Accessories
- Maintenance
- FAQs
- Common Problems
- Buying Guide
- Related Articles

---

## Product Pages

Primary entity

↓

Product

Secondary

↓

Compatible motorcycles

Example

Helmet

Compatible with

Honda CB350

Hunter 350

Classic 350

---

## Internal Links

Link to

Related products

Related motorcycles

Related articles

Related categories

Never create orphan pages.

---

## Writing

Short paragraphs.

Simple English.

Use tables where appropriate.

Use bullets.

Avoid filler.

Avoid AI-sounding phrases.
