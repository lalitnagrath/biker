The Hero Xtreme 160R 4V is a sporty naked motorcycle that combines bold design with a refined 4-valve engine. With its sharp handling, lightweight chassis, and modern features, it delivers an engaging and confident ride for the urban enthusiast.

✗ Not Ideal For
