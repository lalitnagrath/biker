Engine Oil Guide

Choosing the right engine oil is crucial for your motorcycle's performance and longevity.

Types of Engine Oil

  • Mineral: Budget option, needs frequent changes
  • Semi-Synthetic: Good balance of protection and cost
  • Fully Synthetic: Best protection, longer intervals

Recommended Oils

  • Motul 7100 10W-50: Premium fully synthetic
  • Motul 3100 10W-40: Good semi-synthetic option
  • Shell Advance Ultra 10W-40: Reliable choice

How Often to Change

  • Mineral oil: Every 2,000-3,000 km
  • Semi-synthetic: Every 3,000-5,000 km
  • Fully synthetic: Every 5,000-7,000 km
