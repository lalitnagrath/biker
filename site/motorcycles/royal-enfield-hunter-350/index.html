Frequently Asked Questions

What is the price of Royal Enfield Hunter 350?

The Royal Enfield Hunter 350 is priced at ₹1,49,900 (ex-showroom). On-road prices may vary depending on your city and variant.

What is the mileage of Hunter 350?

The Royal Enfield Hunter 350 offers a mileage of approximately 36 kmpl.

Is Hunter 350 good for long rides?

The Hunter 350 is primarily designed for city commuting and short weekend rides. However, with proper accessories like a comfortable seat and windscreen, it can handle moderate long-distance touring.

What are the best accessories for Hunter 350?

Essential accessories for the Hunter 350 include a quality helmet, phone mount, crash guard, and bike cover. For touring, consider adding saddlebags and a tank bag. Check out our complete accessories guide.

How often should I service my Hunter 350?

Regular service intervals for the Hunter 350 are every 3,000-5,000 km or every 3 months, whichever comes first. Always follow the manufacturer's recommended service schedule.
