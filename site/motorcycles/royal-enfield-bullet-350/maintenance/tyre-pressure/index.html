Tyre Pressure Guide

Correct tyre pressure is crucial for safety, fuel efficiency, and tyre life.

Recommended Pressures

Condition Front Rear
Solo 22 PSI 28 PSI
With Pillion 22 PSI 32 PSI
Highway 25 PSI 30 PSI

When to Check

  • Weekly
  • Before long rides
  • When temperature changes significantly

Effects of Incorrect Pressure

Under-inflation: Poor handling, increased wear, higher fuel consumption Over-inflation: Reduced grip, harsh ride, center wear
