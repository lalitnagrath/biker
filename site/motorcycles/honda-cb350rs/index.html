Frequently Asked Questions

The Honda CB350RS is priced at 1,96,500 (ex-showroom). On-road prices may vary depending on your city and variant.

The Honda CB350RS offers a mileage of approximately 35 kmpl.

The CB350RS is primarily designed for city commuting and short weekend rides. However, with proper accessories like a comfortable seat and windscreen, it can handle moderate long-distance touring.

Essential accessories for the CB350RS include a quality helmet, phone mount, crash guard, and bike cover. For touring, consider adding saddlebags and a tank bag. Check out our complete accessories guide.
