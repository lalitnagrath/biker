Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common motorcycle accessory questions

For Indian roads, a full-face helmet from brands like SMK, Steelbird, or Axor is recommended. Look for ISI and DOT certification, proper ventilation, and a comfortable fit. Budget between Rs 3,000 to Rs 8,000 for a good helmet. Our best helmets guide covers options for every budget.

The best engine oil depends on your motorcycle type and usage. Motul 7100 10W-50 is excellent for performance bikes, while Motul 3100 10W-40 suits commuter motorcycles. Always check your owner's manual for the recommended grade. See our best engine oil guide for detailed recommendations.

Lubricate your chain every 500 km for city riding, or every 300 km during highway tours. Use a quality chain lube and always clean the chain before applying fresh lubricant. Check our best chain lubes for top picks.

The best phone mounts are vibration-free models from Bobo, Givi, or SP Connect. They protect your phone's camera from vibration damage while providing a secure hold on handlebars. Our best phone mounts guide reviews all top options.

A portable digital tyre inflator is essential for emergency repairs. Look for models with digital gauges, LED lights, and 12V power compatibility. Inflate your tyres to the recommended PSI (typically 28-32 for rear, 22-25 for front). Our best tyre inflators guide has you covered.

When it comes to riding gear, safety should never be compromised. A quality helmet is the single most important purchase any rider can make. We test and review helmets across price ranges from budget-friendly options under Rs 3,000 to premium international brands. Our guides cover full-face, modular, and open-face options suited for Indian weather conditions.

Motorcycle maintenance is another area where the right products make a significant difference. Our maintenance guides break down exactly what you need and when you need it.
