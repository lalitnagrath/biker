In This Guide

    Best Riding Gloves for Indian Motorcyclists

    Your hands are your most valuable tools when riding. They control the throttle, clutch, and brakes, and in a crash, they're often the first body part to hit the ground. Riding gloves protect your hands from impact, abrasion, weather, and vibration, making them essential gear for every motorcyclist.

    Why Riding Gloves Matter

    Protection

    In a fall, riders instinctively put their hands out. Quality gloves with knuckle protection and palm sliders can prevent severe injuries.

    Grip

    Sweaty or wet hands can slip off controls. Leather and textured palms maintain grip in all conditions.

    Comfort

    Vibration from handlebars causes fatigue on long rides. Padded gloves reduce hand fatigue and numbness.

    Weather Protection

    Wind, rain, and cold affect hand dexterity. Gloves maintain comfort and control in varying conditions.

    Types of Riding Gloves

    Short Cuff

    Lightweight, easy to put on, good ventilation. Best for city riding and short commutes.

    Full Gauntlet

    Extended coverage to protect wrists and forearms. Essential for sport riding and track days.

    Adventure/Touring

    Waterproof, insulated, with maximum protection. Built for long-distance riding in varying conditions.

    Summer/Skeleton

    Minimal coverage with maximum airflow. Mesh construction for hot Indian summers.

    Sizing Guide

    Proper fit is crucial for both comfort and protection:

    1. Measure around the widest part of your palm
    2. Measure from wrist to middle finger tip
    3. Use the brand's size chart to find your size
    4. When trying on, make a fist to ensure flexibility
    5. Check that fingers reach the tips without bunching

    Care and Maintenance

    • Clean leather gloves with leather cleaner and conditioner
    • Machine wash textile gloves on gentle cycle
    • Air dry away from direct heat sources
    • Store flat or on a glove tree to maintain shape
    • Replace after any significant impact

    Conclusion

    Investing in quality riding gloves is non-negotiable for safety-conscious riders. Whether you're commuting through city traffic or touring across India, the right gloves protect your hands, improve comfort, and enhance control. Choose gloves that fit well, offer adequate protection, and suit your riding style.
