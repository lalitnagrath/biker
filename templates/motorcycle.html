Clean + Lubricate Chain Every 500-700 km

Use chain cleaner first, then high-quality lube. Extends chain life by 3x.

Check Tyre Pressure Weekly

Cool tyres only. Front: 22-28 PSI, rear: 28-32 PSI for optimal handling.

Avoid Pressure Washing Wheel Bearings

Water causes corrosion. Manual cleaning only for bearings.

Inspect Brake Pads Every Service
